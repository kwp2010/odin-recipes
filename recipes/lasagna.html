Lasagna Recipe

Description

Homemade meat and tomato sauce lasagna. It's the best lasagna you will ever make!

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic to taste

Steps

  1. Fry the hamburger
  2. Boil the noodles
  3. Add noodles to pan
  4. Layer in tomato sauce, hamburger, and cottage cheese
  5. Top with tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, and shredded mozzarella
